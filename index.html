The Momo Mission

Momo's was originally founded in 1995 in Los Angeles, CA by first generation Kim Park, with the help of her parents.
Kim's father always referred to her as “Momo” which translates to peaches; peaches were his favorite and so was Kim.
Kim's father became terminally ill with cancer when she was 19, and shortly two years after his diagnosis, he had passed away in 2002.
Kim and her mother grieved and struggled through their loss. Momo's had met its end.
Kim and her mother gave themselves a fresh start by moving north of California.
Here is where she decided to continue to live out her father's dreams and opened up Momo's Korean Fried Chicken.
It was a hard leap of faith for Kim and her mother and the community met them with the strongest embrace! Since then, the rest was history.
Kim now spends most of her time taking care of her elder mother, and providing her communities with real food, real chicken, made with real love!
Going strong on 10+ years of business, we are dedicated to bringing the comfort of food to you.
